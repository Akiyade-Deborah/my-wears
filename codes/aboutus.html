About Us

Stylish and Comfortable Shoes for Every Occasion

What does My Wears represent? In our opinion, it represents the re-thinking of fashion in a new world changing faster than before.

Using exclusively traditional technologies, we are working on making only such shoes to our customer, which transcending the quickly changing fashion trends serve the comfort and satisfaction of the person wearing them.

Akiyade Deborah, the founder leader of My Wears, founded her enterprise in 2020. By the revival of the rich traditions of the profession, she gave a new impetus to the art of shoemaking in a new age.

Our shoes are made from the finest calfskin, horse leather, and different exotic leathers by careful craft in our Budapest workshop where we prepare our shoes using special shoemaker tools and hundreds of meticulous operations. The handmade perfectly fitting shoes prepared in our workshop meet the highest quality requirements. In case of circumspect shoe care, careful use of shoe trees, and repair in due time, they can serve their wearer for decades.
